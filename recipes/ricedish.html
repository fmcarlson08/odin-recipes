Rice Dish

Description

This rice dish is a mixture of chopped summer sausauge, white onion, bell peppers and seasonings with jasmine rice

Ingredients

  • Jasmine Rice
  • Summer Sausage
  • White Onion
  • Bell peppers
  • Seasonings - cajun, paprika, pepper

Steps

  1. Prepare Jasmine rice by boiling 1 cup of rice in 2 cups of water until rice absorbs the watter.
  2. Chop or dice the summer sausage, white onion and bell peppers.
  3. Put avocado oil in a large saucepan, mix summer sausage, onion and bell peppers in that oil. Add seasoning to this mixture. Cook until ingredients are light brown in color.
  4. Add the completed rice to mixture of summer sausage, onion and bell peppers on same saucepan.
  5. Mix rice and other ingredients until there is an even distribution
  6. Ready to serve
