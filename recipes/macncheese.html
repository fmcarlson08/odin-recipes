Mac and Cheese

Description

A dish of macaroni and a cheese sauce, most commonly cheddar sauce

Ingredients

  • Elbow noodles
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Unsalted Butter
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
  2. Cook elbow noodles by boiling in water until desired texture
  3. Drain the water from the noodles
  4. Mix noodles with cheese, butter, and milk
  5. Put mac and cheese into a casserole dish
  6. Once oven is at 400 degrees, place casserole dish in oven and bake for 10-15 minutes
